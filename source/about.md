I am an [otaku](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Otaku), who loves watching Japanese anime and playing [galgame](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bish%C5%8Djo_game). ## About this blog
I have finally built this blog, which is powered by Hexo and GitHub Pages after some failures because I am NOT a programmer and know little about programming. I built this blog in order to own a platform for me to write articles. Powering my blog by GitHub Pages is a perfert choice because it is totally free and friendly to beginner without programming basis like me. It is luckily for me to find such a beautiful theme named *[anodyne](https://github.com/klugjo/hexo-theme-anodyne)*, made by [Jonathan Klughertz](http://www.codeblocq.com/)-san.

I am going to write some articles to share my knowledge and my life experiences. These article may be written in English (tagged `en`), Simplified Chinese (tagged `zh-CN`), Traditional Chinese (tagged `zh-TW` or `zh-hant`) or Japanese (tagged `ja`). All the articles written by me on my blog are released under [Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International Licence (CC BY-SA 4.0)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/4.0/). If you find some mistakes in my articles, feel free to point it out then I will correct them.

The blog has enabled [Disqus](https://disqus.com/) for comments. But it might not work without connecting through proxy if you are browsing my blog in mainland China because of [Great Firewall](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Great_Firewall).

I hope that you will enjoy my blog. Have fun.　:D
