About me

Hi, welcome to my blog. Born in 1997, I am an university student majoring in chemistry in South China Normal University, Guangdong, P.R.China (🇨🇳). I am an otaku, who loves watching Japanese anime and playing galgame. Besides, I am a Wikipedian with the rights of patroller and rollbacker in Chinese Wikipedia. I am interested in languages and linguistics so I am learning Cantonese, Taiwanese Hokkien and Japanese.

About this blog

I have finally built this blog, which is powered by Hexo and GitHub Pages after some failures because I am NOT a programmer and know little about programming. I built this blog in order to own a platform for me to write articles. Having my blog powered by GitHub Pages is a perfert choice because it is totally free and friendly to beginner without programming knowledge like me. It is luckily for me to find such a beautiful theme named anodyne, made by Jonathan Klughertz-san.

I am going to write some articles to share my knowledge and my life experiences. These article may be written in English (tagged #en), Simplified Chinese (tagged #zh-CN), Traditional Chinese (tagged zh-TW or zh-hant) or Japanese (tagged ja). All the articles written by me on my blog are released under Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 International Licence (CC BY-SA 4.0). If you find some mistakes in my articles, feel free to point it out then I will correct them.

The blog has enabled Disqus for comments. But it might not work without connecting through proxy if you are browsing my blog in mainland China because of GFW (Great Firewall of China).

sm.ms and elimage are adopted for picture hosting on my blog.

I hope that you will enjoy my blog. Have fun.　:D
